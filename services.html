Trekking

A multi-day, long-distance journey on foot through natural, often remote and rugged terrain like mountains, forests, and valleys, focusing on exploring scenery and culture

Hikking

Walking for recreation in natural environments like forests, mountains, or trails, ranging from short day trips to multi-day adventures, offering exercise, stress relief, and a chance to connect with nature and scenery

River Rafting

An adventurous outdoor activity where a team uses paddles to navigate an inflatable raft down a river, often through exciting whitewater rapids

Motorcycle Touring

Long-distance travel by motorcycle, focusing on exploring new places with a deep connection to the journey, offering freedom, adventure, and unique perspectives you miss in cars, often involving route planning, luggage, and specific bikes designed for comfort and carrying gear, blending road riding with potential off-road exploration

Camping

an outdoor recreational activity where people spend one or more nights in a temporary shelter, like a tent or RV, to enjoy nature, disconnect from daily life, and engage in simple living, often involving activities like hiking, fishing, and cooking over a fire for a rejuvenating experience.
